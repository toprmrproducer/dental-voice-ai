Questions worth asking.

If you're still on the fence, these might help.

DentAI is an AI-powered phone receptionist built specifically for dental clinics. It answers every incoming call, handles appointment scheduling, answers patient questions, and integrates directly with your Practice Management Software — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, without breaks or sick days.
No IT team required. Setup takes under 48 hours. You provide your clinic's information (services, pricing, team), choose your AI voice, set your scheduling rules, and forward your phone number to DentAI. Our onboarding team handles everything else.
DentAI integrates with all major dental PMS platforms including Dentrix, Eaglesoft, Curve Dental, Open Dental, Denticon, ClearDent, Dentric, AbleDent, Archo One, and Tracker. Don't see yours? Contact us — we add new integrations regularly.
Most patients have no idea. DentAI uses advanced natural language processing to hold natural, empathetic conversations. You can choose whether to disclose the AI nature upfront. Many clinics keep the name and voice consistent with their brand, and patients simply experience fast, friendly service.
DentAI is trained to recognize emergency language. When a patient describes an emergency (severe pain, trauma, swelling), DentAI immediately escalates the call to your on-call staff, sends an SMS alert, and provides the patient with emergency instructions while they wait.
DentAI is priced per clinic location, with plans starting at a flat monthly rate. There are no per-call fees or usage limits. Book a demo and our team will provide a custom quote based on your clinic's call volume and requirements.
